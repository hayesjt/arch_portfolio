Grocery

Grocery is an extremely complex section of any store. You must consider how people will move through the space, what produce they will go to first, and how to fit so many carts into one place. On top of the dynamics of food shopping you must consider the plumbing, the electrical, and the foods that react with one another. Every detail can make or break a shopping experience.
