Casa do Pego

Casa do Pego is a beautiful modern get away home located in Comporta, approximately a quarter mile away from Pego Beach. This home may be labeled as a one level but the architect, Pedro Ferreira Pinto, made use of the changing elevations, creating the perfect combination of personal and public sectors of the home. Using this piece of architecture my goal was to do an analysis of all its features and break down key concepts on the architecture that make this space significant. I choose to represent these concepts through models, as seen below.
