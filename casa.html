Casa do Pego

Casa do Pego is a beautiful modern get away home located in Comporta, approximately a quarter mile away from Pego Beach. This home may be labeled as a one level but the architect, Pedro Ferreira Pinto, made use of the changing elevation, creating the perfect combination of personal and public sectors of the home. My goal was to do an analysis of all its features and break down key concepts that make this building significant. I choose to represent these concepts through models, as seen below.
